# Community Foundry, 3-Minute Demo Script

## 0:00 to 0:25, the problem

“Experts know what they want to teach, but turning that knowledge into a community people will join, use, and pay for is a messy strategy project. Community Foundry compresses that work into minutes.”

On the landing page, point out the ten high-demand template verticals and the build-from-scratch entry.

## 0:25 to 1:05, idea to business

Enter: “I help women over 40 turn their corporate experience into a consulting business.”

Select **Build my community**. Show the generated foundation, pricing, discussion categories, classroom, and live Skool-style preview. Apply the AI Coach suggestion.

Say: “The AI proposes. The founder decides. Nothing silently replaces their strategy.”

## 1:05 to 1:40, intelligence that changes the plan

Open **Promotion**. The launch score begins at 74 and identifies acquisition as the weak link. Select **Apply referral campaign**. The score moves to 86.

Show the first-25-member plan, referral loop, lead magnet, five social posts, and three-email launch sequence.

Say: “This is not a vanity score. It explains the bottleneck, fixes it, and gives the founder the campaign assets to act.”

## 1:40 to 2:15, brand and market evidence

Open **Brand**. Show the icon, cover, and launch graphic. Generate one variation. With an OpenAI key, `gpt-image-2` creates it. Without credentials, the demo-safe fallback keeps the presentation moving.

Open **Launch** and show cited market signals with dates.

## 2:15 to 2:50, ship it

Download the launch blueprint. Briefly show JSON and the classroom and community CSV exports.

Show the Skool capability report.

Say: “Skool's integration surface is limited, so Community Foundry never fakes support. Safe actions require confirmation, unsupported actions are labeled, and every project can ship through export.”

## 2:50 to 3:00, close

“Community Foundry turns expertise into a community business, not just a page of AI copy. Strategy, curriculum, retention, promotion, brand, and launch, all in one founder-controlled workflow.”

Close on the 86 Launch Score and the download buttons.
